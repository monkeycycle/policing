July 8, 2021 Media Release

Dangerous Operation of Vehicle/Mischief â Investigation: R21-34923

On July 7, 2021, at approximately 6:30 p.m., a truck was observed driving erratically towards the Manitoba Legislative Building. The vehicle proceeded through several construction traffic/safety cones and accelerated up the main entrance stairs before coming to a stop.

The male driver subsequently exited the vehicle in an agitated state and was taken into custody by Winnipeg Police Service members. A female passenger was also arrested.

A 33-year-old male of Winnipeg is facing charges of:

- Dangerous Operation of a Conveyance
- Mischief Under $5,000/Obstruct in Enjoyment

He was released on an Undertaking and admitted to a hospital for a medical assessment.

The female passenger was released without charges.

Homicide â Investigation: C21-157227

On July 7, 2021, at 9:28 a.m., members of the Winnipeg Police Service responded to the Robertson neighbourhood for a report of a family-related abduction that had just occurred.

It is believed that the estranged father of a 3-year-old girl had abducted her from the mother at knifepoint.

At 9:43 a.m., officers took the suspect into custody outside of a parked vehicle at King Edward Street and Jefferson Avenue. Inside the car, officers found the young girl suffering from stab wound injuries and immediately began providing emergency first aid.

The child was transported to hospital in critical condition, where she succumbed to her injuries.

Members of the Homicide Unit continued with the investigation.

Frank Nausigimana, a 28-year-old male of Winnipeg, has been charged with:

- First Degree Murder

He was detained in custody.

Robbery â Arrest: C21-156538

On July 6, 2021, at 12:33 p.m., members of the Winnipeg Police Service responded to a report of an assault in the 300 block of Selkirk Avenue. A male had been seen kicking an elderly male in the head and causing injuries.

Upon arrival, officers located a crowd tending to the injured 72-year-old male. He was subsequently transported to hospital where he remains at this time.

It was determined that the victim had been walking in the area when a lone suspect assaulted him. The victim was pushed to the ground, and the suspect continued to violently assault him, rendering him unconscious, then rummaged through his clothing prior to leaving.

Members of the Major Crimes Unit continued with the investigation, and during the evening of July 7, 2021, a suspect was placed under arrest in the 800 block of Main Street.

Jonathan Eric Joseph McKay, a 21-year-old male of Winnipeg, has been charged with:

- Robbery
- Assault Cause Bodily Harm

He was detained in custody.

Assault with a Weapon â Investigation: C21-157870

On July 7, 2021, at 11:50 p.m., members of the Winnipeg Police Service received a report of an assault near Logan Avenue and Isabel Street.

A 52-year-old female had been walking in the area when she was approached from behind and held at knifepoint. She subsequently suffered injuries when she attempted to stop the attack and was later transported to hospital in stable condition.

Detectives are working on obtaining a suspect description and motive for this incident.

This investigation is continuing by members of the Major Crimes Unit. Anyone with information regarding this matter is asked to contact investigators at 204-986-6219 or Crime Stoppers at 204-786-TIPS (8477).
