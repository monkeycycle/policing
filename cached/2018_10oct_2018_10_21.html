Winnipeg Police Service

October 21, 2018

Winnipeg Police Service Media Release
For Immediate Release

Serious Motor Vehicle Collision: C18-232545

On October 21, 2018, at approximately 5:00 a.m., emergency services personnel responded to a 2-vehicle collision on Pembina Hwy and Bishop Grandin Blvd. A pick-up truck, northbound on Pembina Hwy. collided with a vehicle making a southbound turn from Bishop Grandin Blvd.

The driver of the truck was taken to hospital in critical condition, however, he has since been upgraded to stable. The driver of the second vehicle was taken to hospital in unstable condition but has since been upgraded as well.

The driver of the truck, a 31-year-old male from Winnipeg, was arrested and will be charged with Driving While Ability Impaired (alcohol) Cause Bodily Harm and Refuse to Provide Sample. He was released on a Promise to Appear.

How can we make this web page better?

Information collected will be used to improve our website. Do not use this form to submit a request for service or information because it will not be forwarded to departments for response. To submit a request for service or information, contact 311.

Contact the Corporate Access and Privacy Officer by mail (City Clerk’s Department, Susan A.
